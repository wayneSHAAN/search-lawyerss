Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our legal services

How do I find a lawyer in my state?

Simply select your state from the dropdown menu or click on your state in the state selection section. Our platform will then display all available lawyers in your selected state with their specialties and consultation fees.

What information do I need to provide to consult with a lawyer?

You only need to provide your name, phone number, state, and a brief description of your legal issue. No sensitive documents or detailed personal information is required for the initial consultation request.

How much does a consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary by lawyer and are clearly displayed on each lawyer's profile. Fees typically range from ₹500 to ₹3,000 depending on the lawyer's experience and specialization. Our admin will confirm the exact fee before your appointment.

How long does it take to get an appointment?

Our admin team typically responds within 1-2 business hours. We strive to schedule consultations within 24-48 hours of your request, depending on lawyer availability. For urgent matters, please mention this in your message.

Do I need to create an account to use your services?

No, BetterCallKunz requires no registration or account creation. You can directly search for lawyers, contact our admin, and request consultations without any login process.

Still Have Questions?

If you have a question that isn't answered here, feel free to contact us directly through WhatsApp or our contact form. Our team is ready to assist you with any legal inquiries.
